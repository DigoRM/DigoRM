Here is a curated showcase of my core production-ready applications, representing advanced automation, interactive data engineering, and enterprise analytics:

### 📊 1. OptimaReports · Enterprise AI Video Analytics & Reporting Platform
OptimaReports is a premium, enterprise-grade data compilation and visualization platform designed to transform complex raw operational data from AI-driven video audits into highly standardized, interactive, and client-ready reporting dashboards and deliverables.
*   **Core Function**: Automatically parses operational Excel sheets from video audits, rendering a trilingual executive dashboard (Chart.js) and compiling print-ready PDFs and PowerPoint (PPTX) decks on-the-fly using a headless browser engine.
*   **Value Delivered**: Automates weekly franchise reports in seconds (saving operations hundreds of manual compilation hours), ensures total format standardization, and complies with strict privacy regulations (LGPD/GDPR) by anonymizing store identities on-the-fly.

### 📈 2. PerformaCX · Customer Experience (CX) Performance Analytics
PerformaCX is a high-performance, interactive analytics platform designed to analyze operational support databases and visualize productivity metrics, quality indicators, and throughput in Customer Experience (CX) environments.
*   **Core Function**: Centralizes and parses support ticket databases using Pandas, presenting real-time comparisons of team productivity against custom resolution targets, Average Handling Time (TMA/AHT), and overall Net Promoter Score (NPS) satisfaction trends.
*   **Value Delivered**: Eliminates manual weekly ticket consolidation, provides manager dashboards with individual analyst benchmarking vs. team average, supports dynamic multilingual localization, and generates downloadable daily statistics and PDF reports.
*   **Links**:
    *   🚀 **[Live Web Application](https://mr-marcolino-cx-performance-movi.hf.space)** *(Hugging Face Space)*
    *   📦 **[GitHub Repository](https://github.com/DigoRM/CX_performance_movi)**

### ✉️ 3. AutomateEmails · VIP Outreach & CRM Ticket Automation Suite
AutomateEmails is a sophisticated full-stack React and Node.js application designed to manage, review, and automate high-volume bulk email outreach sequences and Helpdesk ticketing.
*   **Core Function**: Integrates directly with Helpdesk REST APIs to proactively create resolved tickets, inject custom tags, and link customer profiles via private internal comments, while running a rate-limit safe delay queue scheduler.
*   **Value Delivered**: Resolves a major workflow bottleneck in a legacy ERP ecosystem. Instead of agents opening dozens of browser tabs to analyze player records, the app aggregates financial history and executes personalized campaigns in one place. Eliminates manual post-contact spreadsheet logging, allowing operators to export data directly to financial impact worksheets, improving operational scalability.
*   **Links**:
    *   📦 **[GitHub Repository](https://github.com/DigoRM/AutomateEmails-HelpdeskAPI)**
